# User Personas — ORMC AI Document Audit

**Source:** [Discovery: AI Document Audit](https://nusinfoplace.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/nusit/pages/1578860576/Discovery+AI+Document+Audit)
**Extracted:** 2026-04-21

---

## Persona 1: Principal Investigator (PI)
**Role:** Primary user — performs document audits
**Context:** Faculty members responsible for laboratory safety compliance. Currently reads through large sets of audit documents manually and tracks findings in separate spreadsheets.
**Goals:**
- Quickly review and annotate large document sets
- Ask AI questions about document content to retrieve facts
- Summarize and cross-reference audit documents efficiently
- Export findings and annotations for compliance reporting
**Pain Points:**
- Reading large document sets is extremely time-consuming
- Manual tracking in spreadsheets is disconnected from source documents
- Risk of missed findings and inconsistent reviews
**Frequency:** Regular (during audit cycles)

## Persona 2: PI Supporting Staff
**Role:** Day-to-day user — assists PIs with audit tasks
**Context:** Administrative or research staff who support the PI in preparing, organizing, and reviewing audit documents.
**Goals:**
- Upload and organize documents for PI review
- Assist with annotation and findings documentation
- Prepare export packages for compliance submissions
**Pain Points:**
- Manual document organization and preparation is labour-intensive
- Difficulty tracking which documents have been reviewed
**Frequency:** Regular (during audit cycles)

## Persona 3: ORMC Staff (Office of Risk Management and Compliance)
**Role:** Stakeholder / Business Owner — governs and influences audit process
**Context:** ORMC oversees the audit process, defines compliance criteria, and reviews audit outcomes. They do not perform audits directly but may review and manage audit dossiers.
**Goals:**
- Ensure audit processes meet compliance standards
- Review and approve audit findings
- Access shared dossiers for oversight
**Pain Points:**
- No centralized system for monitoring audit status
- Limited visibility into PI audit activities
**Frequency:** Periodic (review cycles)

## Persona 4: System Administrator
**Role:** [Assumed] Configures system settings, AI agents, and tagging prompts
**Context:** IT or designated administrative staff responsible for system configuration including AI agent prompts, tagging criteria, and user management.
**Goals:**
- Configure AI agent system prompts for specific audit activities
- Manage document tagging prompts and criteria
- Manage system settings and user access
**Pain Points:**
- Need clear administrative interface for non-technical configuration
**Frequency:** Occasional (setup and maintenance)
